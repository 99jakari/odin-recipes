BANANA PUDDING

If you're looking for a wonderfully delicious sweet treat for you and your family to enjoy, Banana Pudding is a year-round dessert you can try!

Ingredients you will need:

  • Cream Cheese
  • Sweetened Codensend Milk
  • Instant Vanilla Pudding
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cool Whip
  • Nilla Wafers
  • Ripe Bananas

Instructions to make:

  1. In a large bowl, mix in your 5 oz. pudding mix and 2 cups of milk.
  2. Blend in the condensed milk until the batter is smooth.
  3. Stir in the vanilla extract
  4. Fold in the whipped topping
  5. Layer wafers, bananas, and pudding mixture into a large serving bowl
  6. Chill your finished Banana Pudding in the frdige until ready to serve!
